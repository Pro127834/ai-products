Health & Fitness Wearables

AI-based wearables track activity, heart rate, sleep patterns, stress, and recovery. Devices like Apple Watch, Samsung Galaxy Watch, Fitbit, and Oura Ring analyze real-time body data to provide personalized fitness and wellness insights.

Apple Watch Series 9 / Ultra

Apple Watch uses advanced health sensors to measure heart rate, ECG, blood oxygen, temperature trends, and sleep quality. With crash detection, fall alerts, and fitness tracking, it works as a powerful daily health companion. AI helps detect irregular heart rhythms and gives personalized fitness coaching. Ideal for iPhone users looking for medical-grade health monitoring. Price: ₹41,000 – ₹89,900

Samsung Galaxy Watch 6

Samsung Galaxy Watch 6 tracks sleep score, body composition (BIA), stress levels, ECG, and heart rate with strong AI-driven insights. Smooth UI, strong battery life, and deep integration with Samsung Health make it excellent for Android users. Price: ₹29,000 – ₹55,000

Fitbit Charge 6

Fitbit focuses heavily on wellness data — heart rate zones, sleep score, breathing rate, stress monitoring, and workout tracking. With a lightweight design and long battery life, it's a budget-friendly health wearable. Its AI health guidance system provides recovery suggestions and daily readiness scores. Price: ₹11,000 – ₹15,000.

Oura Ring Gen 3

Oura Ring targets sleep, recovery, and wellness more than workouts. It analyses heart rate variability (HRV), temperature, and night movement to provide accurate sleep staging and recovery insights. Compact and discreet alternative to watches. Price: ₹28,000 – ₹35,000.
