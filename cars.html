AI in Mobility

Electric scooters use smart dashboards, GPS, OTA updates, and AI-driven riding data for safer and efficient travel.

Ather 450X

Touchscreen display, Google Maps navigation, fast charging, high acceleration, strong build, OTA updates. Range approx 110–145 km. Best handling in segment. Price: ₹1.28 lakh – ₹1.58 lakh

Ola S1 Pro

Longer range up to 181 km, voice commands, touchscreen, multiple modes, keyless entry, and AI-enabled battery management. Ideal for city commute. Price: ₹1.25 lakh – ₹1.40 lakh

Tesla Model 3 Autopilot

Handles highway lane-keeping, braking, and speed automatically. Best consumer-level autonomous tech but still requires supervision. Constant OTA upgrades improve capability. Price: ₹28–50 lakh equivalent (not in India officially)

Waymo Driver System

Fully driverless taxis operating in select US cities. Uses LiDAR + neural AI to drive without humans. A next-generation transportation system still in testing phase. Price: Not sold to public
