Smart Speakers & Voice Assistants

Smart speakers like Amazon Echo and Google Nest allow hands-free interaction with AI-powered assistants. They manage smart homes, control lights, answer questions, and even play your favorite music using natural language processing.

Amazon Echo Dot (5th Gen)

Clearer audio, improved bass, and faster response time. Works seamlessly with smart lights, plugs, switches, and routines like morning alarms and weather updates. Best for Alexa users. Price: ₹4,499 – ₹5,499

Google Nest Mini (2nd Gen)

Superior voice recognition and tight integration with Google services like YouTube Music, Calendar, Maps, and Chromecast. Accurate responses and natural conversation tone make it ideal for Google ecosystem users. Price: ₹3,499 – ₹4,999
